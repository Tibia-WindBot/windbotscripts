[CENTER][SIZE=7][B]Recent Loot[/B][/SIZE]

[IMG]http://i.imgur.com/vrjrOcJ.png[/IMG][/CENTER]


[SIZE=4][B]Description[/B][/SIZE]

It'll show all the recent loot and highlight the important ones.


[SIZE=4][B]Configuration[/B][/SIZE]

Change [I]MaxLines[/I] to the number of lines you want to display.
Change [I]ShowNothing[/I] to [B][COLOR="#0066ff"]true[/COLOR][/B] to show "nothing" messages on the display, set it to [B][COLOR="#0066ff"]false[/COLOR][/B] to hide them.
Fill [I]Items[/I] with the item names you want to display.
Change [I]Scroll[/I] to [B][COLOR="#0066ff"]true[/COLOR][/B] to enable mouse scroll between messages on the display, set it to [B][COLOR="#0066ff"]false[/COLOR][/B] to ignore this feature.
Change [I]Length[/I] to the maximum text length you want to display.
