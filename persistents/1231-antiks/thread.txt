[CENTER][SIZE=7][B]Anti KS (Kill Steal)[/B][/SIZE][/CENTER]


[SIZE=4][B]Description[/B][/SIZE]

Avoid killing monsters that doesn't belong to your character while hunting.


[SIZE=4][B]Configuration[/B][/SIZE]

Change the [I]MinDist[/I] variable to specify the distance limit to kill monsters. For Knights set this option to 1 or 2, for other vocations put 3 - 6. You can add characters names you want to steal by putting their names on the [I]SafeList[/I] table.
